Difference between Margin, Border and Padding in HTML

In this blog post, I will explain Margin, Border and Padding in HTML and their difference. They are word used in HTML which is a language used to build web page. When a content is presented on a web page, Margin, Border and Padding determine space around the content, border around the content and inside space between border and actual content.

What is Margin, Border and Padding

  • Margin

    • Margin is space between border and end of web page or other content in each direction (left, right, top, bottom). in the figure 1, margin is coloured in Orange.

  • Border

    • Border is lines around content element, border is coloured in yellow in the figure 1.

  • Padding

    • Padding is space between content and border, Padding is coloured in green in the figure 1.


Difference

The difference between Margin and Padding is location of space to set. and broder is a line (could be thick or thin or broken line) wrapping around content not like Margin and Padding
